# Product Decision

## Differentiation

wrapminal turns local agent history into evidence-backed stories of how you got stuck, recovered, and verified the result—not another dashboard of tokens and costs.

## Metric presentation

**Resolved Loops:** Present one shareable terminal-style card with a single headline count (`7 loops escaped`), a concise `stuck → recovery → verified` trace, and a playful label; generate it only when the local session logs contain enough evidence.
